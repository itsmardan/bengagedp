Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for Bengaged Media

Introduction

This Privacy Policy embodies our dedication to safeguarding privacy and ensuring the confidentiality and security of personal information. At Bengaged Media, we specialize in digital marketing services, underlining our commitment through transparent privacy practices outlined in this document.

Use of Information

Personal information collected by Bengaged Media is used solely for the purpose of delivering targeted digital marketing solutions. Our aim is to fulfill our clients’ specific needs, ensuring data relevance and necessity for the provision of our services.

Data Confidentiality and Security

We prioritize the confidentiality and security of client data above all else. Data is securely stored in advanced cloud infrastructure, protected by leading-edge security measures including sophisticated encryption and firewalls, to prevent unauthorized access and ensure data integrity.

Data Collection Limitations

Our data collection is strictly limited to what is essential for our operational effectiveness. We consciously avoid collecting or retaining sensitive personal identification information beyond what is required for the scope of our digital marketing services.

World-Class Data Security Measures

Bengaged Media employs the highest standard of data security measures, incorporating robust encryption and cutting-edge technology to safeguard client information, establishing a secure and trustworthy data environment.

Data Retention Policy

Our policy on data retention is minimalist, aiming to retain data only for as long as it is necessary for our service provision. We do not retain sensitive information unnecessarily and advocate for secure digital transactions to minimize data storage on our systems.

User Rights

In line with GDPR and the UK Data Protection Act, individuals have rights regarding their data, including but not limited to accessing, correcting, and requesting the deletion of their personal data. We accommodate all such requests as per regulatory requirements, facilitated through the contact details provided below.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

Bengaged Media reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy periodically. The most current version will always be available on our website. We encourage individuals to review this policy regularly to stay informed on how we protect personal information.
